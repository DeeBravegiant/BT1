# Q1733: BIP340 Schnorr public-private blob downgrade in schnorr_2p.cpp

## Question
Can an unprivileged attacker enter through `coinbase::api::ecdsa_mp::sign_ac` with ac_key_blob, access_structure, msg, sig_receiver, and malicious peer messages during backup verification before recovery, reach `src/cbmpc/protocol/schnorr_2p.cpp` `sign`, and use scalar-detached public blob edited to look like a full signing key blob to bypass the requirement that redacted blobs are tagged and rejected by sign/refresh until attach succeeds, causing signing or refresh uses absent, stale, or attacker-supplied private scalar and producing an in-scope cb-mpc bounty impact?
