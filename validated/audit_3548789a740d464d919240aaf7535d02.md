## Can `from_bin` Accept Infinity?

**For secp256k1:** `ecurve_secp256k1_t::from_bin` delegates to `secp256k1_eckey_pubkey_parse`, which rejects the infinity encoding (`0x00` prefix) and returns an error. The attack fails at deserialization for secp256k1. [4](#0-3)
