### Title
Stale TEE Attestation Accepted at Protocol Use Time — `is_caller_an_attested_participant` Does Not Re-Verify Attestation Validity - (File: crates/contract/src/tee/tee_state.rs)

---

### Summary

The `is_caller_an_attested_participant` function, which gates every critical protocol method (`vote_pk`, `vote_reshared`, `start_keygen_instance`, `start_reshare_instance`, `respond`, `respond_ckd`, `respond_verify_foreign_tx`), only checks that a stored attestation *exists* and that the account/key identity matches. It never calls `re_verify()` on the stored attestation to confirm the attestation has not expired or that the node's image hash is still on the allowed list. This is the direct analog of the OCSP Stapling gap: revocation/expiry status is checked only at submission time, not at use time.
