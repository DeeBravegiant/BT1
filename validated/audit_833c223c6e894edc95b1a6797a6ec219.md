Based on my research, I've found the following:

- `ProposalInit.proposer` **is** validated in `single_height_consensus.rs` against the committee-elected proposer
- `ProposalInit.builder` ("Address of the one who builds/sequences the block") is **never** validated in `is_proposal_init_valid`
- `builder` flows directly into `convert_to_sn_api_block_info` → `ValidateBlockInput.block_info` → batcher execution, setting the sequencer address for the block
