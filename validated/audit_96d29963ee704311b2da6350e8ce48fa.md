### Summary

The Dijkstra era introduces `DirectDeposits` — a new transaction body field (`Map AccountAddress Coin`) that credits coin directly to registered account balances. However, `DirectDeposits` is absent from both the `consumed` and `produced` value calculations used in the ledger's value conservation check. Any transaction submitter can include arbitrarily large `DirectDeposits` in a Dijkstra transaction, causing those amounts to be credited to target accounts without being deducted from the transaction's
