Direct Deposits Omitted from Produced-Value Accounting Enables Unbounded ADA Creation - (File: `eras/dijkstra/impl/src/Cardano/Ledger/Dijkstra/UTxO.hs`)

---

### Summary

In the Dijkstra era, the `directDepositsTxBodyL` field is absent from both the produced-value and consumed-value calculations used in the preservation-of-value (PoV) check. Because `applyDirectDeposits` unconditionally credits ADA to staking accounts after the PoV check passes, an unprivileged transaction sender can craft a transaction whose UTxO inputs and outputs are balanced (so the PoV check passes) while simultaneously including arbitrary `DirectDeposits` that are credited to accounts for free — creating ADA out of thin air.
