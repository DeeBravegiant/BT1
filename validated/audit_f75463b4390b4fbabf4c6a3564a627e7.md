### Title
Missing Value Conservation Check in Sub-Transaction UTxO Rule Allows Direct ADA Creation — (File: eras/dijkstra/impl/src/Cardano/Ledger/Dijkstra/Rules/SubUtxo.hs)

---

### Summary

The Dijkstra era's `SUBUTXO` transition rule (`dijkstraSubUtxoTransition`) omits the `validateValueNotConservedUTxO` check that is mandatory in every other era's UTxO rule. An unprivileged transaction sender can craft a sub-transaction whose outputs exceed its inputs in value, creating ADA out of thin air. The top-level `validateValueNotConservedUTxO` check in `dijkstraUtxoTransition` operates only on the pre-sub-transaction UTxO snapshot (`originalUtxo`) and therefore cannot detect the imbalance introduced by sub-transactions.
